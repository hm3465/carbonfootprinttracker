What is my Carbon Footprint?

Your carbon footprint is the total impact your lifestyle has on the environment. It reflects the greenhouse gases released through daily activities such as using energy, traveling, eating, and shopping.

Understanding your carbon footprint helps you recognize how your choices affect the planet and where simple changes can make a real difference towards a cleaner and more sustainable future.
