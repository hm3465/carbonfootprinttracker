What is my Carbon Footprint?

Understanding your carbon footprint helps you see how daily choices impact the planet. Tracking it empowers you to make sustainable decisions for transportation, energy use, and diet.

Every action—like driving, using electricity, or eating meat—produces greenhouse gases. By identifying where most of your emissions come from, you can reduce waste and contribute to a cleaner environment.
