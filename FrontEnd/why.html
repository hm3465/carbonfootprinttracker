Why Your Carbon Footprint Matters

Every action we take, from driving a car to using electricity at home, produces CO₂ emissions that contribute to climate change.

By tracking your carbon footprint, you can understand the impact of your daily activities and make better choices to reduce emissions.

Small steps, like using public transport or switching to renewable energy, can collectively make a big difference.
