How to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Reducing your carbon footprint can be simple if you focus on the areas where you consume the most energy.

Transportation

  • Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transit whenever possible.

Energy

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and consider renewable electricity sources.

Diet

  • Eat more plant-based meals and reduce meat consumption to lower emissions.

Tracking your daily activities with our carbon footprint tracker helps you identify areas for improvement and measure your progress.
